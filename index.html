There Wasn't a Word for Any of It

What Virtual Peer Support Does for Native American Women Veterans

A reported feature on Televeda's women Veterans peer support program, created with the Arizona Department of Veterans' Services. It follows Agnes Nez, who drives to a ridge on the Navajo Nation each Thursday night to find enough signal to join. The story focuses on access, trust, and what practical support looks like in rural communities.
